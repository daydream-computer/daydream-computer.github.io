the lowercase, tilde.town, and our values

inconsistent lowercase is great because it's easier to type in the terminal, and it's more accessible to people whose main focus isn't english punctuation and grammar. by removing the requirement to type in perfect punctuation and grammar, we take away any elitism associated with it. this; however, doesn't mean you have to type in lowercase.

lowercase is also the unwritten spirit of tilde.town, where we all met.

tilde.town is built around kindness, respect, intentional community, and inclusiveness. these are values we wish to see not only in our community, but also on the internet as a whole.
